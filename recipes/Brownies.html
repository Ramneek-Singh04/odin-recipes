Brownies - An American Classic Dessert

Ingredients: (1 Cup = 240 mL)

  • Cocoa Powder
  • Butter
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Eggs
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat Oven to 345 Degrees
  2. Melt Butter in Saucepan
  3. Stir in Sugar, Vanilla, and Salt
  4. Let the Saucepan cool down, test with your hand
  5. Add the cold eggs, one at a time, and stir vigorously
  6. Add the rest of the dry ingredients
  7. Stir in any extra toppings
  8. Spread evenly in baking pan
  9. Bake and enjoy
