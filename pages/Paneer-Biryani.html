Paneer Biryani

Paneer Biryani, a very popular dish all across India and the world. This savory dish captures the heart of everyone.

Ingredients (1 Cup = 240 mL)

In order to cook the rice you will need:

  • 2 Cups of Aged Basmati Rice
  • Salt (To Taste)
  • Cooking Oil
  • Lemon Juice

The spices you need are:

  • 1 to 2 Bay Leaves
  • 2 Star Anise
  • 6 Green Cardamoms
  • 2 inch cinnamon pieces
  • 2 Black Cardamoms
  • 1 Strand Mace

    • For the gravy:

  • Paneer
  • Cooking Oil (Ghee Preferred)
  • 2 Cups Onions
  • Thai Chilli Pepper
  • 1.5 Tablespoon Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1/2 Cup Pureed Tomatoes
  • Kashmiri Chilli Powder
  • Biryani Masala
